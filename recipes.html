Signature Dish

Margherita Pizza

Classic Neapolitan pizza with fresh basil and mozzarella

Serves 2-3 Prep: 30 mins | Cook: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough - 500g
  • San Marzano tomatoes - 400g
  • Fresh mozzarella - 200g
  • Fresh basil - 1 bunch

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C
  2. Stretch dough to 30cm diameter
  3. Spread crushed tomatoes
  4. Add mozzarella and basil
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes

Equipment

  • Baking tray
  • Rolling pin
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon
Signature Dish

Butter Chicken

Creamy Indian curry with tandoori-spiced chicken

Serves 4 Prep: 20 mins | Cook: 40 mins

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs - 800g
  • Garam masala - 2 tbsp
  • Heavy cream - 200ml
  • Tomato puree - 150g

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken in spices
  2. Sauté onions and garlic
  3. Add tomato puree and cook
  4. Simmer with cream
  5. Serve with naan

Equipment

  • Large skillet
  • Mixing bowls
  • red Choping board(if chicken is not pre cut)
  • knife(if chicken is not pre cut)
  • Blender (for sauce)

Mediterranean Salad

Fresh summer salad with feta and olives

Serves 2 Prep: 15 mins | Cook: 0 mins

Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes - 200g
  • Cucumber - 1
  • Feta cheese - 100g
  • Kalamata olives - 50g

Instructions

  1. Chop vegetables
  2. Combine in bowl
  3. Add olive oil dressing
  4. Top with feta

Equipment

  • Chopping board
  • knife
  • Mixing bowl
  • Wooden spoon
